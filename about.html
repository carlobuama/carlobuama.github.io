More about me

General Information

I am a Filipino-Canadian, born and raised in Canada by immigrant parents. I've lived in Canada my whole life, though I have traveled to Italy, the United States and the Philippines a handful of times. To my own disappointment I don't speak Tagalog, but I speak fluent English and have the core French FSL certificate from high school.

Extracurriculars and Hobbies

Throughout high school, I was in my school's jazz and orchestral band, where I played tenor saxophone, alto saxophone and piano. In grade 12, I joined my school's DECA team with my friend and made it to Provincials. I love playing music and consistently took piano lessons since I was 5 years old and have placed top 3 in a handful of local piano competitions.

Currently, my interests include playing competitive FPS games (Valorant, Counter Strike 2), osu!, music transcription, mechanical keyboard modding, watching anime (my favourite anime of all time is Steins;Gate ), PC building, and cycling when the weather is nice. I really enjoy JRPG games such as the Persona series and Honkai Star Rail. At TMU I participate in some TMU.gg (TMU Gamer's Guild) events including their keyboard meetup in 2023, where I was a quarter-finalist in their typing competition. I also actively participate in bootcamps and workshops offered by TRSM to strengthen my skills in various softwares and programming languages.
