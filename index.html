A bit about my current program

My current program is the Business Technology Management (BTM) program. This program teaches about the fundamentals of business, technology and management and the skills relevant in pursuit of rapidly growing career paths in those fields, such as Business Intelligence and Information Technology.

My favourite course

Among the variety of courses offered in BTM, a personal favourite of mine is ITM618: Business Intelligence and Analytics. This course put my Python coding skills to the test with the use of the Pandas library to find insights in a real data set. As someone looking into pursuing a career in Data Analytics, I believe that the skills I gained in this course will be extremely relevant in my future endeavours.
