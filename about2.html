General Information

I am a Canadian of Filipino descent. I was born in Canada and raised in Toronto by my hardworking parents. My mom immigrated from Singapore and my dad immigrated from the Philippines in the 90s. I have lived in Toronto my whole life, though I've been fortunate to have the opportunity to travel to Italy, the United States, and the Philippines a few times. I can speak English natively and I can probably speak French well enough to get by. Sadly, I can't speak Tagalog but I think I can understand it pretty well.

I love technology and I really enjoy tinkering with electronics as a hobby. I am always excited to mess around with outdated technology like desktop computers to turn them into something useful for myself. One of my favourite ongoing projects is my NAS server built using an old Dell Studio 435mt that I picked up from an e-waste sale. I found that working on these projects and making mistakes has been the easiest way for me to learn about building systems and learning how they work.

I am equally passionate about music, whether it be playing or listening to it. I have been immersed in music since I was a kid, starting off with piano lessons at the age of 6 and picking up clarinet, saxophone and bass guitar later on. I have a few trophies from piano competitions from my childhood, and though I unfortunately no longer take lessons for piano, I still love to jam out to random songs I learned by ear. I hope to learn trumpet one day so I can get more noise complaints.
